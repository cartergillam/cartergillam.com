    Hey there, I’m Carter — a Software Developer, a Queen’s University Computing Major, and a bit of a jack-of-all-trades with a love for tech, design, and creativity.

But here's the twist: for the past three summers, I traded my keyboard for power tools, working in renovations, where I honed my attention to detail and learned the art of making things not just work — but look incredible.
