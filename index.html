    Hey there, I’m Carter — a Software Developer, a Queen’s University Computing Major, and a bit of a jack-of-all-trades with a love for tech, design, and creativity.

    By day, I’m building sleek, high-performing web apps using JavaScript (React & Redux), crafting intuitive UI/UX designs, and solving problems with Python, C++, Java, and more. But here's the twist: for the past three summers, I traded my keyboard for power tools, working in renovations, where I honed my attention to detail and learned the art of making things not just work — but look incredible.
